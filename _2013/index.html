ME233 Advanced Control Systems II, UC Berkeley, Spring 2013¶

ME233 discusses advanced practical control topics, including but not limited to: Linear Quadratic Optimal Control, Kalman Filter, Linear Quadratic Gaussian Problem, Loop Transfer Recovery, System Identification, Adaptive Control and Model Reference Adaptive Systems, Self Tuning Regulators, Repetitive Control, Disturbance Observers.

  • Instructor: Masayoshi Tomizuka, Department of Mechanical Engineering, UC Berkeley.
  • Room 5100B Etcheverry Hall (E.H.), 642 0870, tomizuka AT me DOT berkeley DOT edu
  • Office Hours: Tu and Th 1:00 - 2:00 pm in 5100B E.H.
  • Teaching Assistant: Xu Chen, Department of Mechanical Engineering, UC Berkeley.
  • Room 2103 E.H., 642-0935, maxchen AT me DOT berkeley DOT edu
  • Office Hours: Wed 1:00 pm - 2:30 pm in 136 Hesse, F 5:00 pm - 6:00 pm in 1165 E.H. (campus map)
  • Lectures: Tu, Th 11:00 am - 12:30 pm in Room 3109 Etcheverry Hall
  • Discussion: F 4:00-5:00 pm in 1165 Etcheverry Hall
  • Class Notes: ME233 Class Notes (Parts I and II) by M. Tomizuka, Part I can be purchased at Copy Central, 48 Shattuck Square
  • Online course schedule: can be found from http://schedule.berkeley.edu/
