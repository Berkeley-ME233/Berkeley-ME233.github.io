Logistics¶

Prerequisites¶

ME 232 or its equivalent

Grading¶

40 % from 2 midterms (open one page summary sheet for each test), 40 % from final (open notes) and 20 % from assigned homework.

References¶

Probability¶

  1. D.P. Bertsekas, Introduction to Probability, Athena Scientific
  2. R.D. Yates and D.J. Goodman, Probability and Stochastic Processes, second edition, Willey
    1. Poolla, Probability notes

Linear Quadratic Optimal Control¶

  1. B.D.O. Anderson and J.B. Moore, Optimal Control: Linear Quadratic Methods, Dover Books on Engineering (paperback), 2007. A PDF can be downloaded from: http://users.rsise.anu.edu.au/%7Ejohn/papers/index.html
  2. Frank L. Lewis, Vassilis L. Syrmos, Optimal Control, Wiley-IEEE, 1995
    1. Bryson and Y-C. Ho, Applied Optimal Control: Optimization, Estimation, and Control, Wiley

Stochastic Control Theory and Optimal Filtering¶

    1. Grover Brown and P. Hwang, Introduction to Random Signals and Applied Kalman Filtering, Third Edition, Willey
  2. Frank L. Lewis, L. Xie and D. Popa, Optimal and Robust Estimation, Second Edition CRC
    1. Grewal and A. Andrews, Kalman Filter, Theory and Practice, Prentice Hall
  4. B.D.O. Anderson and J.B. Moore, Optimal Filtering, Dover Books on Engineering (paperback), New York, 2005. A PDF can be downloaded from: http://users.rsise.anu.edu.au/%7Ejohn/papers/index.html
  5. K.J. Astrom, Introduction to Stochastic Control Theory, Dover Books on Engineering (paperback), New York, 2006

Adaptive Control¶

  1. Astrom, K.J. and Wittenmark, B., Adaptive Control, Addison Wesley, 2nd Ed., 1995
  2. G.C. Goodwin and K.S. Sin, Adaptive Filtering Prediction and Control, Prentice Hall, 1984
    1. Sastry and M. Bodson, Adaptive Control: Stability, Convergence, and Robustness, Prentice Hall, 1989. (Book can be downloaded from http://www.ece.utah.edu/~bodson/acscr/)
    1. Krstic, I. Kanellakopoulos, and P. V. Kokotovic, Nonlinear and Adaptive Control Design, Willey
